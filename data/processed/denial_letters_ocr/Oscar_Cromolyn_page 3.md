We received an authorization request from your prescriber for coverage of **CROMOLYN 100 
MG/5 ML ORAL CONC** for you. Unfortunately, that request was denied. We received an appeal 
of this denial on **02/14/2026**. However, the appeal has also been denied due to the 
following reason(s) not being met:

We have received a request for the treatment of mast cell activation. Based on the 
information provided, this request was not approved. Your doctor did not provide a reason 
why the requested medication is being appealed. We have reviewed the new information 
submitted with this request.

Your current records with us and the information submitted by your doctor do not meet 
the following criteria:

- documentation supporting a condition of **systemic mastocytosis** (too many mast cells, 
  a type of white blood cell, building up in your body)

- documentation this medication is being used to manage skin, gastrointestinal, neurological, 
  and nasal (nose) and ophthalmic (eye) symptoms associated with **systemic mastocytosis**

Please talk with your provider about the treatment for mast cell activation.

We recommend you reach out to your doctor to discuss this information and treatment 
alternatives.
